Learn More About Harmonia Sinfonia

Welcome to Harmonia Sinfonia, Cheshire's premier amateur orchestra. Our mission is to create a vibrant and inclusive community where musicians of all levels can come together to share their passion for music, improve their skills, and perform beautiful orchestral works.

Background

Founded in 2005, Harmonia Sinfonia has grown into a dedicated group of musicians who are committed to bringing classical music to life. We hold regular rehearsals and performances throughout the year, featuring a diverse repertoire that spans from the Baroque period to contemporary compositions.

Our Mission

At Harmonia Sinfonia, we believe in the power of music to unite and inspire. Our mission is to:

  • Foster a love of music: Provide opportunities for musicians to explore and perform a wide range of orchestral works.
  • Encourage musical growth: Offer a supportive environment where members can improve their skills through regular rehearsals and professional guidance.
  • Engage with the community: Share our passion for music through public performances, educational outreach, and community events.
  • Promote inclusivity: Welcome musicians of all backgrounds and skill levels to join us in our musical journey.

Join Us

We are always looking for new members to join our orchestra. Whether you are a seasoned musician or just starting out, Harmonia Sinfonia offers a place for you to grow and perform. Our members range from students and amateur musicians to retired professionals, all united by a common love for orchestral music.

If you are interested in joining Harmonia Sinfonia, please sign up for more information about auditions and membership.

Upcoming Events

We host a variety of events throughout the year, including seasonal concerts, community performances, and special workshops. Check our events page for the latest updates and join us for an upcoming performance.
